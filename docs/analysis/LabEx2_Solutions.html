Lab Exercise 2 Solutions (Total Marks: 25)¶

Deadline for submission: Two weeks from your lab session (eg, Tuesday/Friday $\rightarrow$ 11:59 pm of Tuesday/Friday two weeks later) Rename your file as AXXXXXXXY_LabEx2.ipynb, where AXXXXXXXY is your matric number.

Question 1:

Create a simple advertising brochure as shown below. The first few lines of codes have been given to you. Consider using "\t" and/or empty spaces for your layout. Also format() to input the discount (30%) and computed discounted price in your output.

Question 2:

Write simple programs (one cell for each part) to solve the following:

(a) Given $x = 10$ and $y = x^3 + 2 \ x + 10$, solve for $y$.

(b) Given $x = 15$ and $y = x\{x[x \ (x^2 + 2)]\} + 10$, solve for $y$.

(c) Evaluate $V(r)$ given user-input values of $\sigma$ and $r$: $$ V(r) = \bigg( \frac{\sigma}{r} \bigg)^{12}- \bigg( \frac{\sigma}{r} \bigg)^{6} $$
